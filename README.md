## Project Overview

This project is a Python-based chess game featuring:
- The **classic chess variant**
- A **fairy chess variant** with new custom pieces

---

## How to Launch the Game

To start the game, simply run the following Python file:

python game.py

## Settings

After launching the game, you can choose between two variants :

-Standard chess rules and pieces


-Fairy Chess Variant, this variant introduces new fairy pieces with special movements:

A Nightrider : Repeats the knight’s movement multiple times in the same direction
<img width="75" height="72" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/506a7b9a-69f9-498c-aa7b-a741c8970a60" />

Bishop-Knight Hybrid : Combines the movement of a bishop and a knight
<img width="72" height="74" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e25f63af-59ab-4cb7-a0cf-7f8278803eda" />

Rook-Knight Hybrid : Combines the movement of a rook and a knight
<img width="72" height="71" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7ce61dc4-0ebe-486a-8b7b-7036b38f6811" />


You can then choose between three different modes:

-Player vs Player :

Two human players
Both White and Black are controlled manually

-Player vs AI (Solo) :

Play against an AI
You can choose to play as White or Black

-AI vs AI :

Watch two AIs play against each other
